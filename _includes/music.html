Never Gave Up On Me is Kristen Faith’s first single, inspired by life events that took her down to her lowest place which led to her most impactful come back. Her Pastor, at the time, would constantly share the message “God is not mad at you, but MAD ABOUT YOU.” That message would echo in her mind as she navigated hurt, condemnation, and shame after yet another fail in her life. Never Gave Up On Me is her anthem to those years spent chasing acceptance and perfection.
